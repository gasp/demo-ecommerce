Our award-winning 500ml Ocean Bottle is designed to come along with you at home and beyond. It keeps your cold drinks cold and your hot drinks hot. Ice, soup, coffee, and smoothies are all good, thanks to its double lid that lets you sip, fill, pour and clean with joy. Our bottles are made from double wall vacuum-insulated stainless steel and recycled materials, won't leak or sweat in your bag and are 100% dishwasher safe‚ all while helping to save the ocean.

Make your bottle uniquely yours with a custom engraving. Maximum letter count: 12. Symbols and emojis will not be engraved.

TOP FAQs

What is the bottle made of?

Recycled stainless steel, BPA-free plastic, silicone rubber and ocean-bound plastic. All materials are fully recyclable.

What are the dimensions of the bottle?

Capacity: 500ml
Diameter: 68mm
Height: 220mm

How long does shipping usually take?

We deliver to most countries using tracked and signed methods. Delivery to the UK is typically within 2-3 days and costs £4.95.Europe delivery takes between 3-6 days and the rest of the world between 5 to 9 days. Note customs and local tax for international orders may be charged separately which are not included in the costs.

How do I clean my bottle?

For daily use we recommend a quick hand wash. Your Ocean Bottle is dishwasher safe up to 60°C. Sometimes dishwashers leave watermarks, don’t panic, simply dip your Ocean Bottle in really hot water (approx 70°c) for 3-5 seconds.

Do you have replacement parts available for purchase?

We’ve got you covered for life with our repair & recycling scheme in case you need new spare parts or something breaks. To activate, simply register your bottle at oceanbottle.co/register.
