What happens next?

When you buy an Ocean Bottle, we fund the collection of 1000 ocean-bound plastic bottles in weight. This collection provides a sustainable living for people in coastal communities and, together, we all keep plastic out of the ocean.

But it doesn’t stop there.

Your Ocean Bottle contains a chip you can scan at special readers around the world (we’re adding more all the time). Each time you refill your bottle, we’ll collect even more plastic before it reaches the ocean. The plastic we collect gets recycled, so it can be made into something new. That’s not just a win-win situation, it’s a win-win-win… win?
